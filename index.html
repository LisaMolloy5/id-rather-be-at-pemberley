About The Club

Who are We?

We are the 'I'd Rather Be At Pemberley Book Club'. A group of avid readers and Jane Austen lovers.

When do we meet?

We meet on the first Wednesday of every month over zoom. Zoom invites are sent out a day prior to the meeting.

What do we do?

We meet once a month to review a book in the regency romance genre.

Who is welcome?

If you love Jane Austen and can't read enough romance books set in the regency era you have found the right place.All are welcome wether you can name every Jane Austen heroine or can't tell your Miss Bennets from your Miss Dashwoods.
