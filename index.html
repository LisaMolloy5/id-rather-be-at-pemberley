About The Club

Do you love Jane Austen? Could you spend hours debating that Colin Firth is the best Darcy or that the 2005 Pride and Prejudice is actually the best adaptation of this classic book then you came to the right place. We are an online book club who meet review books all about regency and romance. Everyone is welcome whether you know every character in Sense and Sensibility or if you don't know your Mr.Tilneys from you Mr. Knightleys. Every month we pick a new title to read and then meet on the first wednesday of every month for a nice chat over zoom. We are based in Dublin but no matter where in the world you are we invite you to give your opinions on all things regency. Head over to our join page and become apart of this great club.
