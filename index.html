About The Club

Who are We?

We are the I'd Rather Be At Pemberley Book Club. A group of avid readers and Jane Austen lovers.

When do we meet?

We meet on the first wednesday of every month over zoom. Zoom invites are sent out a day prior to the meeting.

What do we do?

We meet once a month to review a book in the regency romance genre.

Who is welcome?

Do you love Jane Austen? Could you spend hours debating that Colin Firth is the best Darcy or that the 2005 Pride and Prejudice is actually the best adaptation of this classic book then you came to the right place.
